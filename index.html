Frequently Asked Questions

Is Windows 12 compatible with my current hardware?

Windows 12 is designed to support a wide range of modern hardware. Minimum requirements include a 64-bit processor, 8GB RAM, and 50GB free storage.

Can I upgrade from Windows 11 to Windows 12?

Yes, a seamless upgrade path is available for Windows 11 users with compatible devices. Your files and apps will be preserved.

What new features does Windows 12 offer?

Windows 12 introduces AI-powered assistance, immersive VR support, instant updates, and enhanced cloud integration among many other innovations.

Is my data safe with Windows 12?

Absolutely. Windows 12 features multi-layered AI-driven security protocols to protect your data and privacy at all times.

How often will Windows 12 receive updates?

Updates are delivered instantly in the background with zero downtime, ensuring your system is always secure and up to date.
